Just a mini side quest that is giving me temporary fulfillment while I battle with my survival guilt feelings being away from my hometown. Super glad and grateful to be here in London but at the same time I feel very powerless and I feel the strong urge to give back to my Lebanese community.

{/* truncate */}

I want to help my family, friends, and my people back home. So here's collegesaurus, the proper and simplified guide for college applications in Lebanon.

It would be cool to gather all the Lebanese college application information in one place, but it would be much cooler to create a network of university alumni guiding and helping high schoolers in their career journey.
