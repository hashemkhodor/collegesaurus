# Lebanese International University (LIU)

## Overview

- **Founded:** 2001
- **Type:** Private
- **Education Type:** American credit-based model
- **Campus(es):** 9 campuses in Lebanon — Beirut, Bekaa (main), Saida, Nabatieh, Mount Lebanon, Tripoli, Tyre, Rayak, and Halba-Akkar. International campuses in Yemen, Senegal, and Mauritania.
- **Language of instruction:** English
- **Websites:**
  - Main: [liu.edu.lb](https://liu.edu.lb/LIU/)
  - Admissions: [liu.edu.lb — Admission & Registration](https://liu.edu.lb/NewLIU2022/AR/index.php)
  - Financial Aid: [liu.edu.lb — Financial Aid](https://liu.edu.lb/NewLIU2022/admission/financialaid.php)
  - Apply online: [applynow.liu.edu.lb](https://applynow.liu.edu.lb/)

The Lebanese International University was established in 2001 as "The Bekaa University"
under Presidential Decree No. 5294, with its first campus in Al-Khyara in the western
Bekaa Valley. It was founded by Abdul Rahim Mourad, former Lebanese Minister of Defense
and Education. After opening a second campus in Beirut in 2002, it was renamed to
Lebanese International University. LIU has since grown to nine campuses across Lebanon
and three abroad, making it one of the largest private universities in the country by
geographic reach. The university is organized into five schools and offers around 58
programs at the undergraduate and graduate levels.

### Requirements

#### SAT

LIU does **not** require SAT scores for admission. Admission is based on the Lebanese
Baccalaureate (or equivalent) and university placement tests (UPTs).

#### High-school grades

- A recognized secondary school certificate is required: Lebanese Baccalaureate II (any strand), Technical Baccalaureate (BT3), or an equivalent foreign credential as recognized by the Lebanese Ministry of Education and Higher Education.
- School of Education applicants must have an overall average of at least **50%** on their Baccalaureate II or third-year secondary exams.
- An equivalence from the Lebanese Ministry of Education is needed for non-Lebanese curricula.

Reference: [LIU Admission Requirements](https://liu.edu.lb/LIU/view_page_content.php?page_name=QWRtaXNzaW9uIFJlcXVpcmVtZW50cw%3D%3D&template_id=Mw%3D%3D) |
[School of Education — Admissions](https://sites.google.com/liu.edu.lb/schoolofeducation-liu/academics/admissions/undergraduate-admissions)
