## Application

### Application Types + Windows

AUB offers several application tracks. **Early Merit** applicants are evaluated
first and may receive merit scholarships; **Regular** is the main window;
**Spring** admission is available for limited seats; and **Transfer** is for
students with university credits.

**Difference between tracks:**

- **Early Merit** — Apply by October 31. Requires top 25% class rank and
  higher SAT thresholds. Decisions arrive by end of December. Early Merit
  recipients are automatically considered for the 30% / 25% / 20% merit
  scholarships (Early Admission / Early Action / Early Decision respectively).
- **Regular** — The main application window (November – December 20).
  Standard SAT minimums apply. Decisions by end of March.
- **Spring** — For mid-year enrollment. Limited seats, same requirements as
  Regular. Not all programs accept Spring applicants.
- **Transfer** — For students who have completed university-level courses.
  Students with fewer than 54 credits must still submit SAT scores. Students
  with 24+ credits (junior transfer) are **waived from the SAT**. Course
  syllabi required for credit evaluation.

**SAT waiver:** Applicants with an overall average of 13/20 or equivalent in both
Grade 10 and Grade 11 may be waived from the SAT requirement. Junior transfer
students (24+ credits) are also waived.

### High-school grades

- Official transcripts for Grades 10, 11, and 12 are required.
- Early Merit applicants must rank in the **top 25%** of their class.
- A recognized secondary school certificate is required (Lebanese
  Baccalaureate, IB, French Bac, A-Levels, US High School Diploma, etc.).
- An equivalence from the Lebanese Ministry of Education is needed for
  non-Lebanese curricula.
- Hard-copy transcripts and diploma must be submitted by early July before
  enrollment.
