### Requirements to join
- **Lebanese Baccalaureate:** required for most faculties; minimum averages and
  eligible streams vary by faculty.
- **Equivalence:** non-Lebanese curricula must obtain a Ministry of Education
  equivalence certificate first.
- **Concours:** required for selective faculties.
- **Language:** depends on the faculty's language of instruction.
