# Université Saint-Joseph de Beyrouth (USJ)

## Overview

- **Founded:** 1875
- **Type:** Private Catholic research university (Jesuit)
- **Education Type:** French/European model (ECTS credit system — Licence–Master–Doctorat)
- **Campus(es):**
  - **Beirut (5 campuses):** Medical Sciences (Damascus Street), Sciences & Technology (Mar Roukouz), Social Sciences "Huvelin" (Monnot Street), Humanities (Damascus Street), Innovation & Sports (Damascus Street)
  - **Regional centers:** Sidon (South Lebanon), Zahlé (Beqaa Valley), Tripoli (North Lebanon)
  - **International:** USJ-Dubai (UAE)
- **Languages of instruction:** Trilingual — French, English, and Arabic (varies by program)
- **Websites:**
  - Main: [usj.edu.lb](https://www.usj.edu.lb)
  - Admissions: [usj.edu.lb/admission](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/demande.php?lang=2)
  - Financial Aid: [usj.edu.lb/aide](https://www.usj.edu.lb/aide/?lang=2)
  - Apply online: [admission.usj.edu.lb](https://admission.usj.edu.lb/)

Université Saint-Joseph de Beyrouth was founded in 1875 by French Jesuit missionaries,
making it one of the oldest universities in the Middle East. It is the only university
in the Arab world following the European Credit Transfer System (ECTS). USJ currently
enrolls approximately 12,650 students served by 2,000 academic staff across 13 faculties,
14 institutes, and 9 schools. The university operates Hôtel-Dieu de France hospital and
houses the Bibliothèque Orientale research library. USJ was reaccredited by ACQUIN
(without conditions) for eight years in 2025. Graduates include seven Lebanese presidents
and numerous government officials. USJ celebrates its 150th anniversary in 2025.

## Majors

USJ is organized into 13 faculties with 14 institutes and 9 schools. Programs follow the
LMD system (Licence 3 years / Master 2 years / Doctorate 3 years). The language of
instruction varies by program — French, English, or Arabic.

## Application

### Application process

Undergraduate applications are submitted **online** at [admission.usj.edu.lb](https://admission.usj.edu.lb/)
or at physical submission offices on campus. Candidates can choose up to **9 programs**
from the list, ranked in order of preference.

The application fee is **$100 USD** (non-refundable), paid once and valid for all three
admission periods plus the spring semester of 2026-2027.

Selective programs (Medicine, Dental Medicine, Pharmacy) require an **entrance exam**.
Candidates for FM, FMD, and FP are allowed to take the entrance exam **at most twice**
(consecutively or not).

Reference: [USJ Admissions](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/demande.php?lang=2)

### Requirements

#### SAT

USJ does **not** require SAT scores. Admission is based on the Baccalaureate and,
for selective faculties, the entrance exam.

#### High-school grades

- **Early Admission:** Transcripts for 1st and 2nd Secondary (Grades 10 & 11)
- **Regular / Late Admission:** Transcripts for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd Secondary (Grades 10, 11 & 12)
- Certified summary of academic transcripts (signed by the school)
- Certified copy of the Lebanese Baccalaureate or recognized equivalent from the Ministry of Education
- Any prior university diplomas or transcripts (if applicable)

Reference: [Required Documents](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/documents.php?lang=2)

#### English proficiency

USJ is trilingual (French/English/Arabic). Language requirements depend on the chosen
program's language of instruction. The admissions page does not list specific TOEFL/IELTS
minimums — proficiency is typically assessed through the entrance exam or program-specific
requirements.

## Tuition & Scholarships

### Tuition

USJ uses the European Credit Transfer System (ECTS). A full academic year is 60 credits.

| Level | Approximate annual cost (USD) | Reference |
| ----- | ----------------------------- | --------- |
| Bachelor programs | ~$10,920/year | [UniPage — USJ](https://www.unipage.net/en/20105/saint_joseph_university) |
| Master programs | From ~$3,480/year | [UniPage — USJ](https://www.unipage.net/en/20105/saint_joseph_university) |

Tuition is the same for Lebanese and international students. Costs vary significantly
by faculty — medical programs are at the higher end ($6,000–$20,000/year).

### University-offered scholarships

USJ allocated **$28 million USD** for financial aid in 2024-2025, covering **64% of students**
(6,492 recipients). Aid is 30% of the university's total budget.

#### Merit scholarships (Bourse d'excellence)

**Lebanese Baccalaureate students:**

| National rank | Coverage |
| ------------- | -------- |
| 1st nationally | 100% of tuition |
| 2nd nationally or 1st regionally | 70% of tuition |
| 3rd nationally or 2nd–3rd regionally | 30% of tuition |

**French Baccalaureate students:**

| Overall score | Coverage |
| ------------- | -------- |
| ≥ 19.5/20 | 100% of tuition |
| 18.5–19.4/20 | 70% of tuition |
| 17.5–18.4/20 | 30% of tuition |

**Conditions:** Must rank in top 10% of their year to renew. Apply within 30 days of
Baccalaureate results to bureaudurecteur@usj.edu.lb.
