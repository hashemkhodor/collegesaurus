# Université Saint-Joseph de Beyrouth (USJ)

## Faculty

USJ offers ~54 undergraduate programs across 13 faculties, 14 institutes,
and 9 schools, organized under the LMD/ECTS system (Licence 3 yr / Master
2 yr / Doctorat 3 yr). Engineering programs ("Diplôme d'Ingénieur")
run 5 years and 300 ECTS; Architecture is 5 yr / 342 ECTS; Medicine is
7 yr; Pharmacy and Dental Surgery are 5 yr. Instruction is primarily in
French, with a growing number of English-medium programs and Arabic
programs at ILO and FSR. Each table lists the program, degree type,
home department, credits, and duration (years).

## Application

### Application Types + Windows

USJ runs **three undergraduate admission periods** per academic year. The
admission file is filed at the [Service d'Information et d'Orientation
(SIO)](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/) at any campus or online via
[admission.usj.edu.lb](https://admission.usj.edu.lb/). The $100
application fee is non-refundable but is **paid only once** for all three
periods plus the spring semester of the same academic year.

| Period | Opens | Closes | Decisions | Reference |
| ------ | ----- | ------ | --------- | --------- |
| Early (anticipée) | Oct 15, 2025 | Dec 17, 2025 | Rolling, before Bac results | [USJ Admissions](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/) |
| Regular | Feb 2, 2026 | Jun 19, 2026 | Rolling, after Bac results | [USJ Admissions](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/) |
| Late (tardive) | Jun 20, 2026 | Aug 21, 2026 | Late summer | [USJ Admissions](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/) |

**Difference between tracks:**

- **Early (anticipée)** — Apply before Lebanese Baccalaureate results.
  Conditional admission is granted pending final Bac scores. Up to 9
  ranked program choices.
- **Regular** — Main admission window after Bac results. Up to 9 ranked
  program choices. Most applicants apply during this period.
- **Late (tardive)** — Limited seats. **Only one program choice
  permitted.** Higher language thresholds may apply (see Requirements
  below).

Reference: [USJ Admission Conditions](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/conditions.php?lang=2) |
[Application Form](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/demande.php?lang=2)

## Scholarships

### Tuition (AY 2025-26)

USJ uses the European Credit Transfer System (ECTS); a full academic
year is 60 credits. Tuition is the same for Lebanese and international
students and varies significantly by faculty (medical and engineering
programs are at the upper end of the range). USJ does not publish a
plain-text per-credit fee schedule; the official rate sheet is hosted
as a PDF at [tarifs.pdf](https://www.usj.edu.lb/pdf/admission/tarifs.pdf).
Prospective students should confirm current rates with the admissions
office.

| Level | Approximate annual cost (USD) | Reference |
| ----- | ----------------------------- | --------- |
| Bachelor / Licence programs | ~$3,480 – $10,920/year | [UniPage — USJ](https://www.unipage.net/en/20105/saint_joseph_university) |
| Master programs | From ~$3,480/year | [UniPage — USJ](https://www.unipage.net/en/20105/saint_joseph_university) |
| Medicine, Dental Surgery, Pharmacy, Engineering | $6,000 – $15,000+/year | [TopUniversities — USJ](https://www.topuniversities.com/universities/saint-joseph-university-beirut-usj) |

> Contact [orientation@usj.edu.lb](mailto:orientation@usj.edu.lb) /
> +961-1-421128 for the current per-credit rate at your target faculty.

### University-offered scholarships

In AY 2024-25 USJ disbursed a **total of $28M** in scholarships and
financial aid covering **64% of students** — equivalent to roughly 30%
of the university's annual budget.

## Requirements

### SAT

USJ does **not** require SAT scores for admission. Admission is
based on the Lebanese Baccalaureate (or equivalent), an interview, and
USJ's internal language aptitude tests (TALF + English Aptitude Test).

### High-school grades

- A recognized secondary school certificate is required: Lebanese
  Baccalaureate II (any strand), French Baccalauréat, IB Diploma, A-Levels,
  US High School Diploma, or another credential recognized by the
  Lebanese Ministry of Education.
- An equivalence from the Lebanese Ministry of Education is required
  for non-Lebanese curricula.
- Selective faculties (FM, FMD, FP, ESIB, INCI) require additional
  entrance examinations specific to the program.

Reference: [USJ Admission Conditions](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/conditions.php?lang=2)

### Language proficiency

USJ administers its own **Test d'aptitude en langue française (TALF)**
and **English Aptitude Test (EAT)**. Programs are categorized by
language of instruction; candidates are classified into Category A
(stronger) or Category B (weaker but conditionally admissible).

| Test | Threshold | Programs / Notes | Reference |
| ---- | --------- | ---------------- | --------- |
| TALF | Category B (regular period) | French-medium selective faculties: FM, FMD, FP, ESIB, INCI, ETIB, FDSP | [Conditions](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/conditions.php?lang=2) |
| TALF | Category A (late period) | FDSP, ETIB, ESIB, INCI, Nutrition (late period requires stronger TALF) | [Conditions](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/conditions.php?lang=2) |
| English Aptitude Test, or **TOEFL iBT 60–78**, or **IELTS 6.0** | Category B | English-medium programs (regular period) | [Conditions](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/conditions.php?lang=2) |
| English Aptitude Test, or **TOEFL iBT 80+**, or **IELTS 6.5** | Category A | Translation program (late period) | [Conditions](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/conditions.php?lang=2) |

Candidates admitted in Category B must reach Category A before the end
of their first year. Programs **exempt** from the French test include
ILO and ISSR Arabic programs and several English-taught degrees.

Reference: [USJ Admission Conditions](https://www.usj.edu.lb/admission/conditions.php?lang=2)
