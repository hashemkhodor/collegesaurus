# Universities in Lebanon — Overview

Welcome. This tab is your starting point for choosing and applying to a
university in Lebanon. Each university listed here has its **own self-contained
page**, so once you pick a school you can read everything you need without
hopping between articles.

## How each university page is organized

Every university page follows the same five sections, in the same order, so you
can compare schools side by side:

1. **Overview** — general information, founding year, campuses, language of
   instruction, notable alumni, and the latest rankings.
2. **Majors** — most popular and most common majors offered, grouped by faculty,
   with references to the official program pages.
3. **Application** — application types, application windows and deadlines,
   application fees, and the **requirements to join** (SAT range, high-school
   grades, English proficiency, program-specific extras).
4. **Tuition & Scholarships** — tuition cost overview plus all scholarships
   offered *by the university itself* (merit, financial aid, need-based grants,
   work-study, athletic and talent awards).
5. **Contacts** — phone numbers and emails for the admissions office, financial
   aid office, and individual departments.

> Looking for **external** scholarships from NGOs, foundations, or government
> programs? Those live in the [Scholarships](/scholarships/intro) tab.
