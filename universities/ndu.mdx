# Notre Dame University – Louaize (NDU)

## Faculty

NDU offers **59 undergraduate programs** across 7 faculties.

Three campuses: Main at Zouk Mosbeh (Keserwan), North Lebanon at
Barsa-Koura, and Shouf at Deir El Qamar.

English is the primary language on the American credit system (~30
credits/year). NDU is institutionally accredited by NECHE, with ABET
for engineering and computer science and AACSB for business.

## Application

### Application Types + Windows

NDU offers Fall, Spring, and Summer admission rounds. **Early Admission**
applicants who complete admission ahead of the regular deadline receive
a **20% Early Admission Scholarship** (subject to a $500 non-refundable
deposit by March 31, 2026, credited to tuition).

| Term | Opens | Closes | Entrance Exam | Decisions | Reference |
| ---- | ----- | ------ | ------------- | --------- | --------- |
| Fall 2026-27 | March 8, 2026 | June 2, 2026 (12:00 pm) | June 5, 2026 | June 29, 2026 | [NDU Admissions Overview](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/admissions/overview) |
| Spring 2026 | — | — | January 20, 2026 | — | [NDU Admissions Overview](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/admissions/overview) |

**Difference between tracks:**

- **Early Admission** — apply before the regular deadline; receive an
  automatic **20% Early Admission Scholarship**, a $500 non-refundable
  deposit by March 31, 2026 secures the seat (credited to first
  semester's tuition). Combinable with other awards up to 70% total
  coverage.
- **Regular Admission** — main window; June 2 deadline for Fall.
  Applicants without standardized test scores take the NDU Entrance
  Exams (English Entrance Test + Math Aptitude Test) on campus on June 5.
- **Spring** — mid-year intake; entrance exam on January 20, 2026.

Reference: [NDU Admissions Overview](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/admissions/overview) |

## Requirements

### SAT

The SAT is required for all freshman applicants per the Lebanese
Ministry of Education and Higher Education (MEHE):

| Track | Minimum SAT | Reference |
| ----- | ----------- | --------- |
| Arts track | 870 | [NDU Admissions](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/admissions) |
| Sciences track | 950 | [NDU Admissions](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/admissions) |

> Per-faculty / per-program SAT minimums beyond the MEHE thresholds
> are not published publicly. NDU provides a Faculty Requirements PDF
> via the Office of Admissions — contact admission@ndu.edu.lb for
> faculty-specific cutoffs.

**SAT waiver:** Applicants without SAT scores can take the **NDU
Entrance Exams** (English Entrance Test + Math Aptitude Test) on
campus. The Fall 2026 entrance exam date is **June 5, 2026**.

### High-school grades

- Official transcripts for **Grades 10, 11, and 12**.
- A recognized secondary school certificate is required: Lebanese
  Baccalaureate, French Bac, Technical Bac, IB Diploma, US High School
  Diploma, etc.
- An equivalence from the Lebanese Ministry of Education is required
  for non-Lebanese curricula.
- Minimum baccalaureate grades trigger automatic merit scholarships
  (see Scholarships above).

### English proficiency

NDU operates in English. Applicants must demonstrate English
proficiency by one of the following:

| Test | Accepted | Notes | Reference |
| ---- | -------- | ----- | --------- |
| TOEFL iBT | Yes | Official scores submitted | [NDU Admissions](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/admissions) |
| IELTS Academic | Yes | Official scores submitted | [NDU Admissions](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/admissions) |
| NDU English Entrance Test (EET) | Yes | Taken on campus if no standardized test | [NDU Admissions](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/admissions) |

> Specific minimum TOEFL / IELTS scores and EET placement levels are
> not published on the NDU admissions overview. Applicants who do not
> meet the minimum threshold are placed in the **Intensive English
> Program** before starting regular coursework. Contact
> admission@ndu.edu.lb for current cutoffs.
