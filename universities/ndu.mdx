# Notre Dame University–Louaize (NDU)

## Overview

- **Founded:** 1987 (as Louaize Center for Higher Education in 1978)
- **Type:** Private, non-profit Catholic university (Maronite Order of the Blessed Virgin Mary)
- **Education Type:** American model (credit-based)
- **Campus(es):**
  - **Main Campus:** Zouk Mosbeh (Keserwan) — ~34,000 m² built area, ~60 acres, capacity ~8,000 students
  - **North Lebanon Campus (NLC):** Barsa, Koura
  - **Shouf Campus (SC):** Deir El Qamar
- **Language of instruction:** English
- **Websites:**
  - Main: [ndu.edu.lb](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/)
  - Admissions: [ndu.edu.lb/admissions](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/admissions)
  - Financial Aid: [ndu.edu.lb/scholarship-and-financial-aid](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/scholarship-and-financial-aid/new-student)
  - Apply online: [sis.ndu.edu.lb/oar](https://sis.ndu.edu.lb/oar)

Notre Dame University–Louaize is a private, non-profit Catholic university founded in
1987 under Presidential Decree No. 4116. It is the only Catholic university in Lebanon
and the Arab Middle East using the American educational system. NDU traces its roots
to the Louaize Center for Higher Education, established in 1978 by the Maronite Order
of the Blessed Virgin Mary. The university serves approximately 7,000 students across
three campuses with around 205 full-time and 298 part-time faculty. NDU offers 76 major
fields of study across nine faculties. NDU holds full accreditation from the New England
Commission of Higher Education (NECHE, 2018), ABET accreditation for its engineering
programs (since 2016), and AACSB International accreditation for its business faculty (2022).

### Requirements

#### SAT

SAT is required per MEHE (Lebanese Ministry of Education) for freshman students:

| Track | Minimum SAT | Reference |
| ----- | ----------- | --------- |
| Arts track | 870 | [Admissions](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/admissions) |
| Sciences track | 950 | [Admissions](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/admissions) |

> TODO: Per-faculty/per-program SAT minimums are not published publicly. NDU provides
> a Faculty Requirements PDF on their admissions page — contact admissions for details.

**SAT waiver:** Applicants without SAT scores can take the NDU Entrance Exams
(English Entrance Test + Math Aptitude Test) on campus.

#### High-school grades

- Official transcripts for Grades 10, 11, and 12
- Lebanese Baccalaureate or equivalent (IB, French Bac, Technical Bac, US High School Diploma, etc.)
- Equivalence from the Lebanese Ministry of Education required for non-Lebanese curricula
- Minimum baccalaureate grades trigger automatic merit scholarships (see Tuition & Scholarships)

#### English proficiency

NDU operates in English. Applicants must demonstrate proficiency by one of the following:

| Test | Accepted | Notes | Reference |

| NDU English Entrance Test (EET) | Yes | Taken on campus if no standardized test | [Admissions](https://www.ndu.edu.lb/admissions) |
