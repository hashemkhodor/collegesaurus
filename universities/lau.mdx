## Application

### Application Types + Windows

LAU accepts applications for **Fall**, **Spring**, and **Summer** semesters.
Applications are submitted online at [lau.edu.lb/apply](https://www.lau.edu.lb/apply/)
or via [Common App](https://www.commonapp.org/explore/lebanese-american-university-lau/).

| Term / Type | Closes | Notes | Reference |
| ----------- | ------ | ----- | --------- |
| Undergraduate — Fall 2026 (Late) | July 15, 2026 | Main undergraduate window | [Deadlines](https://www.lau.edu.lb/apply/deadlines.php) |
| Undergraduate — Summer 2026 | April 30, 2026 | — | [Deadlines](https://www.lau.edu.lb/apply/deadlines.php) |
| Merit Scholarship — Fall 2026 | January 31, 2026 | Must apply early for merit consideration | [Deadlines](https://www.lau.edu.lb/apply/deadlines.php) |
| Financial Aid (new students) — Fall 2026 | April 30, 2026 | Opens November 3, 2025 | [Deadlines](https://www.lau.edu.lb/apply/deadlines.php) |
| Athletic Scholarship — Fall 2026 | May 30, 2026 | Opens October 15, 2025 | [Deadlines](https://www.lau.edu.lb/apply/deadlines.php) |
| Medical School — 2026-27 | March 31, 2026 | Regular applications | [Deadlines](https://www.lau.edu.lb/apply/deadlines.php) |

> Note: An offer of admission is valid only for one semester.

**Difference between application types:**

- **Freshman Year** — For students who have completed Grade 12 and hold a high
  school diploma (American, IB, British O-levels + A-level, CEGEP, etc.).
  Must secure permission from the Equivalence Committee before registration.
- **Sophomore Year** — For students who hold a recognized Baccalaureate
  (Lebanese, French, German Abitur, Technical Bac, British 6 O-levels +
  3 A-levels, or two completed CEGEP years).
- **Transfer** — For students with university credits from another institution.
  SAT required if fewer than 12 credits completed. Minimum GPA of 2.50/4.00
  for probationary admission. Course transfers require grade C or above.
- **Special Students** — May take courses without pursuing a degree.
- **Second Degree** — Must complete all requirements of the intended school.

Reference: [LAU Catalogue 2025-26 — Admission](https://catalog.lau.edu.lb/2025-2026/undergraduate/admission.php)

## Requirements

### SAT

LAU has adopted a **test-optional policy** for the Fall 2025 admission cycle.
Applicants are not required to submit SAT or ACT scores to be considered for
admission. However:

- SAT/ACT scores **are still required for transfer students with fewer than
  12 credits**.
- Submitting strong SAT scores can qualify you for the **SAT Scholarship
  (50% of tuition)** — see the Scholarships section above.
- When registering for the SAT, use LAU code **2595**.

Reference: [LAU Catalogue 2025-26 — Admission](https://catalog.lau.edu.lb/2025-2026/undergraduate/admission.php) |
[SAT Sphere — LAU](https://www.satsphere.com/resource/university/lebanese-american-university)

### High-school grades

- Official transcripts from the last three years, signed and sent in a
  sealed envelope directly to the Admissions Office.
- Certified copies of all official certificates or diplomas.
- Photocopy of Family Civil Status record (Lebanese and Arab nationals).
- Recent passport-size color photo and ID/passport photocopy.
- Equivalence from the Equivalence Committee required for non-Lebanese
  curricula before registration.

Reference: [LAU Catalogue 2025-26 — Admission](https://catalog.lau.edu.lb/2025-2026/undergraduate/admission.php)
