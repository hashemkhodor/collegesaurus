# Rafik Hariri University (RHU)

## Faculty

RHU offers **20 undergraduate programs** across **3 colleges** —
College of Arts and Sciences (CAS), College of Business Administration
(CBA), and College of Engineering (CE) — at a single 54,000 m² campus
in Mechref, Damour (20 km south of Beirut). RHU follows the **American
credit system** (~30 credits/year) with English as the language of
instruction. Engineering programs are **ABET-accredited**. Each table
lists the program, degree type, home department, credits, duration
(years), and language of instruction.

## Scholarships

### University-offered scholarships

RHU offers three merit scholarship tracks plus need-based aid and a
sibling discount.

#### Merit scholarships

| Scholarship | Coverage | Eligibility | Conditions | Reference |
| ----------- | -------- | ----------- | ---------- | --------- |
| **Merit Scholarship** | 20–50% of tuition | Academic excellence based on school grades | Automatic — no application required | [Financial Aid](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/financial-aid-and-scholarship-programs) |
| **Competition Scholarship** | 20–50% of tuition | Winners of RHU-organized school competitions | Automatic for competition winners; must meet admission requirements | [Financial Aid](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/financial-aid-and-scholarship-programs) |
| **Athletic Scholarship** | 25–50% of tuition | Demonstrated athletic ability | Submit form to Admissions with coach references and awards record | [Financial Aid](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/financial-aid-and-scholarship-programs) |

#### Need-based aid

| Program | Coverage | Eligibility | Reference |
| ------- | -------- | ----------- | --------- |
| **Financial Aid Fund** | Discount on tuition only (excludes books, housing, repeated courses) | New students with limited resources + academic potential; priority to financially challenged students with strong scholastic aptitude. **Deadline: August 15, 2025.** Apply at [ssf.rafikhaririuniversity.org](https://ssf.rafikhaririuniversity.org/). | [Financial Aid](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/financial-aid-and-scholarship-programs) |

## Requirements

### SAT

The SAT is required for Freshman applicants per the Lebanese Ministry
of Education and Higher Education (MEHE):

| Track | Minimum SAT | Minimum ACT | Reference |
| ----- | ----------- | ----------- | --------- |
| Arts track (Freshman) | 870 | 15 | [Admission Requirements](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/undergraduate/admission-requirements) |
| Sciences track (Freshman) | 950 | 17 | [Admission Requirements](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/undergraduate/admission-requirements) |

### High-school grades

- **Lebanese Baccalaureate** — all tracks accepted (General, Life
  Sciences, Sociology & Economics, Humanities & Literature); remedial
  courses may be required.
- **Technical Baccalaureate** — requires remedial courses in Math,
  Physics, Chemistry, Statistics, and/or Economics depending on major.
- **Foreign Secondary Certificates** (French Bac, IB, German Abitur,
  etc.) — must be validated by the Lebanese Ministry of Education.
- **US High School Diploma** — requires equivalence from the Lebanese
  Ministry of Higher Education.
- **British GCE** — minimum 5 O-Levels + 1 A-Level for freshman;
  5 O-Levels + 3 A-Levels for sophomore.

**Required documents:**

- High school transcripts (last 3 years)
- Diplomas and equivalence certificates
- Civil status records
- ID / passport copies
- Three passport-sized photos
- University transcripts (transfer students only)

Reference: [RHU Admission Requirements](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/undergraduate/admission-requirements)

### English proficiency

All instruction at RHU is in English. Applicants demonstrate
proficiency via one of the following (undergraduate minimums):

| Test | Minimum Score | Reference |
| ---- | ------------- | --------- |
| TOEFL iBT | 80 | [Admission Requirements](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/undergraduate/admission-requirements) |
| TOEFL ITP / In-House | 550 | [Admission Requirements](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/undergraduate/admission-requirements) |
| IELTS Academic | 6.5 | [Admission Requirements](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/undergraduate/admission-requirements) |
| SAT Reading & Writing | 530 | [Admission Requirements](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/undergraduate/admission-requirements) |
| RHU English Entrance Exam | 380 | [Admission Requirements](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/undergraduate/admission-requirements) |

Applicants below thresholds enroll in the **Intensive English
Program** before starting regular coursework.
