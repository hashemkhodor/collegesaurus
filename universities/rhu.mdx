# Rafik Hariri University (RHU)

## Overview

- **Founded:** September 1999
- **Type:** Private, non-profit university (founded by the Rafik Hariri Foundation)
- **Education Type:** American system (credit-based, English instruction)
- **Campus(es):** Mechref, Damour (20 km south of Beirut) — single 54,000 m² campus with 9 main buildings
- **Language of instruction:** English
- **Websites:**
  - Main: [rhu.edu.lb](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/)
  - Admissions: [rhu.edu.lb/admission](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/undergraduate)
  - Financial Aid: [rhu.edu.lb/financial-aid](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/financial-aid-and-scholarship-programs)
  - Apply online: [ungradapp.rafikhaririuniversity.org](https://ungradapp.rafikhaririuniversity.org/)

Rafik Hariri University was established in September 1999 by the Rafik Hariri Foundation
with the mission of providing "affordable excellence in education to all, regardless of
faith or political affiliation." RHU is organized into 3 colleges offering 28+ accredited
programs, including Lebanon's first mechatronics engineering degree, the region's first
MBA in oil and gas management, and the first bachelor of science in engineering
technology in Lebanon. **Approximately 25% of students receive scholarships and
financial aid.** RHU celebrated its 25th anniversary in September 2024. Engineering
programs are ABET-accredited.

References: [About RHU](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/about-rhu) |
[ABET accreditation](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/legacy-campaign/impact-stories/abet-accreditation-a-stamp-of-excellence-for-rhu)

Applicants below thresholds enroll in the **Intensive English Program**.

#### High-school grades

- **Lebanese Baccalaureate:** All tracks accepted (General, Life Sciences, Sociology & Economics, Humanities & Literature); remedial courses may be required
- **Technical Baccalaureate:** Requires remedial courses in Math, Physics, Chemistry, Statistics, and/or Economics depending on major
- **Foreign Secondary Certificates** (French Bac, IB, German Abitur, etc.): Must be validated by Lebanon's Ministry of Education
- **US High School Diploma:** Requires equivalence from Lebanese Ministry of Higher Education
- **British GCE:** Minimum 5 O-Levels + 1 A-Level for freshman; 5 O-Levels + 3 A-Levels for sophomore

#### Required documents

- High school transcripts (last 3 years)
- Diplomas and equivalence certificates
- Civil status records
- ID / passport copies
- Three passport-sized photos
- University transcripts (for transfer students only)

### University-offered scholarships

RHU offers three merit scholarship tracks plus need-based aid. **~25% of students**
receive some form of financial assistance.

#### Merit-based scholarships

| Scholarship | Coverage | Eligibility | Conditions | Reference |
| ----------- | -------- | ----------- | ---------- | --------- |
| **Merit Scholarship** | 20–50% of tuition | Academic excellence based on school grades | Automatic — no application required | [Financial Aid](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/financial-aid-and-scholarship-programs) |
| **Competition Scholarship** | 20–50% of tuition | Winners of RHU-organized school competitions | Automatic for competition winners; must meet admission requirements | [Financial Aid](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/financial-aid-and-scholarship-programs) |
| **Athletic Scholarship** | 25–50% of tuition | Demonstrated athletic ability | Submit form to Admissions with coach references and awards record | [Financial Aid](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/financial-aid-and-scholarship-programs) |

#### Need-based aid

| Program | Coverage | Eligibility | Reference |
| ------- | -------- | ----------- | --------- |
| **Financial Aid Fund** | Discount on tuition only (excludes books, housing, repeated courses) | New students with limited resources + academic potential; priority to financially challenged students with strong scholastic aptitude | [Financial Aid](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/financial-aid-and-scholarship-programs) |
| **Sibling Discount** | 10% discount for one sibling | Two or more siblings enrolled simultaneously (max 2 beneficiaries if 3+ enrolled) | [Financial Aid](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/financial-aid-and-scholarship-programs) |
| **Work-Study Program** | On-campus employment, up to 8 hours/week | Apply with financial need documentation | [Financial Aid](https://www.rhu.edu.lb/admission/financial-aid-and-scholarship-programs) |

**Financial Aid Fund deadline:** August 15, 2025. Apply online at [ssf.rafikhaririuniversity.org](https://ssf.rafikhaririuniversity.org/).
