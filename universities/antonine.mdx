# Université Antonine (UA / Antonine University)

## Overview

- **Founded:** 1996
- **Type:** Private Catholic university (administered by the Antonin Maronite Order)
- **Education Type:** French/European model (ECTS-compatible); trilingual instruction
- **Campus(es):**
  - **Main campus:** Hadat-Baabda, Mount Lebanon (63,500 m²)
  - **Nabi Ayla, Zahle** (Bekaa)
  - **Mejdlaya, Zgharta** (North Lebanon)
- **Languages of instruction:** French, English, Arabic (varies by program)
- **Websites:**
  - Main: [ua.edu.lb](https://www.ua.edu.lb/)
  - Facebook: [uAntonine](https://www.facebook.com/uAntonine/)

Université Antonine was founded in 1996 by the Antonin Maronite Order, a Catholic
religious order founded in Lebanon in 1700. The university enrolls approximately
**5,000 students** across 7 faculties. It operates a main campus in Hadat-Baabda
and two regional campuses in Zahle and Zgharta. UA was accredited by the Swiss Agency
of Accreditation and Quality Assurance (**AAQ**) in 2017.

## Majors

UA organizes its programs across 7 faculties. Programs follow a credit-based system
(ECTS-compatible). Most undergraduate programs are **3 years** (Licence); engineering
is typically **5 years**; medicine/dental medicine follows longer tracks.

### Requirements

#### SAT

UA accepts SAT scores per MEHE freshman minimums (870 Arts / 950 Sciences). Selective
programs (Engineering, Health Sciences, Music) have additional entrance exams.

> TODO: Per-program SAT thresholds not published publicly.

#### High-school grades

- Lebanese Baccalaureate or equivalent (IB, French Bac, etc.)
- Equivalence from the Lebanese Ministry of Education for non-Lebanese curricula
- Music programs require an audition
- Engineering programs require a competitive entrance exam

#### Language proficiency

UA is trilingual. Requirements depend on the program's language of instruction:

- **French:** DELF B2 / TCF B2 or acceptable secondary French grades
- **English:** TOEFL, IELTS, or UA placement test
- **Arabic:** Secondary school record sufficient
