# Université Antonine (UA / Antonine University)

## Faculty

UA offers **25 undergraduate programs** across **7 faculties** at three
campuses: **Hadat-Baabda** (BA, main, 63,500 m²) in Mount Lebanon,
**Nabi Ayla-Zahle** (ZA) in the Bekaa, and **Mejdlaya-Zgharta** (ME)
in North Lebanon. UA follows a **credit-based system** (ECTS-aligned)
with **trilingual instruction** — English, French, and Arabic
sections offered across most programs. The university was accredited
by the Swiss Agency of Accreditation and Quality Assurance (**AAQ**)
in 2017. Each table lists the program, degree type, home department,
credits, duration (years), and language of instruction.

## Scholarships

### University-offered scholarships

**56% of UA students benefit from financial support.** UA discounts
are awarded on the basis of Lebanese Baccalaureate average and family
status, with separate sport scholarships.

#### Merit-based scholarships (Lebanese Baccalaureate)

Discount on first-semester tuition based on official Baccalaureate
results:

| General Average | Discount |
| --------------- | -------- |
| 18.5/20 and above | **100%** discount for the Fall Semester |
| 17.5–18.49/20 | **60%** discount for the Fall Semester |
| 16.5–17.49/20 | **40%** discount for the Fall Semester |
| 15–16.49/20 | **20%** discount for the Fall Semester |

Subsequent semesters earn **academic scholarships** based on the
cumulative UA GPA. Reference: [UA Admissions Guide (PDF)](https://ua.edu.lb/Library/Assets//Gallery/Documents/admissions/ua-admissions-guide.pdf).

#### Sibling grants

| Number of siblings | Discount on additional siblings |
| ------------------ | -------------------------------- |
| 2 siblings | 20% discount on the second |
| 3 siblings | 30% discount on the third |
| 4 or more siblings | 50% discount on the fourth+ |

#### Sport grants

Eligibility: holding a Lebanese record, being a first/second-division
player and a member of any UA team sport, being engaged in individual
sports, being a current Lebanese champion, or holding an
international title. Coverage varies.

#### Financial facilities

- **Staggered payment** of tuition fees over the academic year
- **Reduction of tuition fees** (case-by-case)
- Contact the Financial Aid office for full details.

## Requirements

### SAT

UA accepts **SAT** as a substitute for placement exams in eligible
programs. **SAT-based exemptions** by program type:

| Exemption | Minimum SAT Math |
| --------- | ---------------- |
| Mathematics placement test (Business / Music / FIC / FSS / FPH) | 450 |
| Engineering placement test (Mathematics + Physics — FoE BE) | 600 |
| Mathematics placement test (FoE Computer Science BTech) | 500 |

Per Lebanese MEHE thresholds, freshman applicants need a minimum SAT
of **870 (Arts)** or **950 (Sciences)** to enroll directly into
sophomore-year programs.

### High-school grades

UA admits via three pathways:

- **Admission on file** — applicants with strong high-school grades in
  the relevant subjects (e.g., 12/20 or above in Mathematics for
  business programs) may be exempted from placement exams.
- **Admission on title** — applicants with the Lebanese Baccalaureate
  or equivalent (overall ≥14/20 with ≥12/20 in the program-relevant
  subject) qualify directly.
- **Transfer admission** — students with credited courses at another
  higher-education institution, validated by the Office of Orientation
  and Admissions and the department's Transfer Committee.

Recognized secondary credentials include the Lebanese Baccalaureate,
French Bac, IB, German Abitur, and equivalents recognized by the
Lebanese Ministry of Education.

### Language proficiency

UA is **trilingual** (English, French, Arabic). Most programs offer
both English and French sections, requiring proficiency in the
section's language of instruction.

| Test | Accepted | Notes |
| ---- | -------- | ----- |
| TOEFL iBT | Yes | English-section programs |
| IELTS Academic | Yes | English-section programs |
| SAT (EBRW section) | Yes | English-section programs |
| DELF / DALF | Yes | French-section programs |

Applicants without these scores take the **UA placement test** in the
relevant language. UA offers **on-campus IELTS testing** through its
partnership.

> Specific minimum TOEFL / IELTS / DELF thresholds are not published
> in the public Admissions Guide. Contact admissions@ua.edu.lb for
> current cutoffs.
