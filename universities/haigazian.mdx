# Haigazian University

## Overview

- **Founded:** October 17, 1955
- **Type:** Private, non-profit Armenian Evangelical liberal arts university
- **Education Type:** American liberal arts model (credit-based)
- **Campus(es):** Single urban campus in **Kantari, Beirut** — Webb Building, Mehagian Academic Center, Mugar Building
- **Language of instruction:** English
- **Websites:**
  - Main: [haigazian.edu.lb](https://www.haigazian.edu.lb/)
  - Admissions: [haigazian.edu.lb/admissions](https://www.haigazian.edu.lb/admissions/apply-now/)
  - Academics: [haigazian.edu.lb/academics](https://www.haigazian.edu.lb/academics/)
  - University Catalog: [Catalog 2024–2026 (PDF)](https://www.haigazian.edu.lb/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Catalog-2024-2026.pdf)

Haigazian University was established on October 17, 1955 by the Union of Armenian
Evangelical Churches in the Near East (UAECNE) and the Armenian Missionary Association
of America (AMAA), with foundational support from the Mehagian family of Phoenix,
Arizona. It began as Haigazian College (1955–1992), became Haigazian University College
(1992–1996), and adopted its current name in 1996 after Lebanese Ministry authorization.
The university enrolls approximately **760 students** with around 150 faculty and staff,
and 4,700+ alumni. It has expanded from 13 to **30 majors** across 4 faculties. Notably,
students founded the Lebanese Rocket Society in 1960, which developed the Arab world's
first suborbital rockets. Degrees are recognized by the Lebanese government and the
Association of International Colleges and Universities.

## Application

### Application fees

- **Application fee:** $30 (non-refundable)

### Requirements

#### SAT

| Track | Minimum SAT | Reference |
| ----- | ----------- | --------- |
| Arts track | 870 | [Admissions](https://www.haigazian.edu.lb/admissions/apply-now/) |
| Sciences track | 950 | [Admissions](https://www.haigazian.edu.lb/admissions/apply-now/) |

These minimums align with the Lebanese Ministry of Education (MEHE) freshman program
requirements.

#### High-school grades

- Lebanese Baccalaureate or equivalent (IB, French Bac, US diploma, etc.)
- Equivalence from the Lebanese Ministry of Education for non-Lebanese curricula
- Applicants without SAT scores may take the Haigazian entrance exam

#### English proficiency

All instruction is in English. Applicants demonstrate proficiency via SAT reading/writing,
TOEFL, IELTS, or the university's placement test.
