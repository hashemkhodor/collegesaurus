# Haigazian University

## Faculty

Haigazian offers **~28 undergraduate degree-granting programs** across
**4 faculties** at a single urban campus in **Kantari, Beirut** (Webb,
Mehagian, and Mugar buildings). Founded in 1955 by the Union of
Armenian Evangelical Churches in the Near East and the Armenian
Missionary Association of America, Haigazian follows the **American
liberal-arts credit system**; all instruction is in English. The
Faculty of Business Administration and Economics is **IACBE-accredited**.
Each table lists the program, degree type, home department, total
credits required, indicative duration in years, language of
instruction, and a link to the program's official 2024–2026 catalog
sheet.

## Requirements

### English proficiency

All instruction is in English; every applicant must demonstrate
proficiency via one of: **SAT** (institutional code 0106), **TOEFL ITP /
iBT** (institutional code 4278), **IELTS**, or **G.C.E. English Language**
— each valid for two years. Scores determine the English placement.

| Test | Place into ENG 009 (intensive) | Place into ENG 109 | Exempt — placement test |
| ---- | --- | --- | --- |
| TOEFL ITP (paper) | 450–524 | 525+ | 525+ |
| TOEFL iBT | 45–70 | 71–89 | ≥90 |
| IELTS | 4–5 | 5.5–6 | ≥6.5 |
| SAT Reading & Writing | 360–430 | 440–550 | ≥560 |
| G.C.E. English Language | — | B / C | A |

> Teaching Diploma applicants need a **paper TOEFL ≥525 or equivalent**.

### High-school grades

- Lebanese Baccalaureate or equivalent (IB, French Bac, US diploma,
  GCE A-Levels, etc.).
- Non-Lebanese curricula must obtain an **equivalence certificate**
  from the Lebanese Ministry of Education (MEHE) before applying.
- The MEHE freshman SAT minimums (870 Arts / 950 Sciences) apply to
  applicants entering through the freshman track.
- A signed/sealed **secondary-school recommendation form** covering
  the last three years of grades is required.

### Documents

- Photocopy of ID card or passport
- Recent passport-size photo
- Last-three-years secondary school grades on the recommendation form
- Certified copy of the secondary-school certificate
- Certified copy of MEHE Bac equivalence (foreign curricula)
- Official transcripts of any prior university work
- Permission to join the Freshman class (foreign-program candidates)
