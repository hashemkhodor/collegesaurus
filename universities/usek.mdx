# Holy Spirit University of Kaslik (USEK)

## Faculty

USEK offers **~57 undergraduate programs** across **12 faculties + 2
institutes**, taught primarily in **English** or **French** (some
programs in Arabic at the Faculty of Letters and the Pontifical
Faculty of Theology). The university operates a main campus in
**Kaslik (Jounieh)**, a dedicated **Hboub Medical Campus** for the
Faculty of Medicine, and three **Regional University Centers**
(Chekka in the North, Rmeich in the South, and Zahlé in the Beqaa).
USEK uses a **credit-based modular system** compatible with both the
US/Canadian and European ECTS standards. Several programs are offered
in **two parallel sections** (English / French) — listed as separate
rows where the program slug differs. Each table lists the program,
degree type, home department, credits, duration (years), and language
of instruction.

## Application

### Application Types + Windows

USEK runs **two undergraduate admission tracks** — **Scholarship
Admission** (early; eligible applicants are considered for merit
scholarships) and **Regular Admission** — plus distinct windows for
Engineering and Graduate programs. The application portal is
[apply.usek.edu.lb](https://apply.usek.edu.lb/academic-applications).

| Track | Exam window | Application deadline | Reference |
| ----- | ----------- | -------------------- | --------- |
| Scholarship Admission (undergraduate) | March 14 – May 9, 2026 | **April 29, 2026** | [Admission Requirements](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/admission/online-admission-requirements) |
| Regular Admission (undergraduate) | May 22 – July 22, 2026 | **July 15, 2026** | [Admission Requirements](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/admission/online-admission-requirements) |
| Engineering (competitive entrance exam) | June 4 & July 14, 2026 | **July 6, 2026** | [Admission Requirements](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/admission/online-admission-requirements) |
| Graduate | June 2 & July 23, 2026 | **July 15, 2026** (results July 28, 2026) | [Admission Requirements](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/admission/online-admission-requirements) |

**Difference between tracks:**

- **Scholarship Admission** — apply by April 29, 2026 to be considered
  for USEK's competitive merit scholarships (administered alongside
  the entrance exam).
- **Regular Admission** — main window for general undergraduate
  applicants; deadline July 15, 2026 for Fall 2026-27.
- **Engineering** — separate competitive entrance exam (≥75/100 to
  pass) on June 4 or July 14, 2026.

## Requirements

### SAT

USEK accepts the **SAT** as a substitute for its own entrance exams
in eligible programs. Per Lebanese MEHE thresholds, freshman applicants
need a minimum SAT of **870 (Arts)** or **950 (Sciences)**.

**Program-specific exemptions:**

- **Math entrance exam** — pass at ≥70/100 to be exempted.
- **Engineering entrance exam** — pass at ≥75/100 (required for FI
  admission).

### High-school grades

- A recognized secondary school certificate is required: Lebanese
  Baccalaureate, French Bac, IB, etc.
- An equivalence from the Lebanese Ministry of Education is required
  for non-Lebanese curricula.
- Some programs accept the SAT as a substitute for entrance tests.

> Specific minimum baccalaureate averages per faculty are not
> published publicly. Contact admissions for direct-entry
> requirements.

Reference: [Admission Requirements](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/admission/online-admission-requirements)

### French proficiency

USEK is bilingual; many programs are taught in French or are
French-track sections. French proficiency is demonstrated by either:

- **DELF B2** or **TCF B2**, or
- An **average of ≥10/20** in French during Grades 10 and 11.
