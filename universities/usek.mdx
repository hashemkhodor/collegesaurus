# Holy Spirit University of Kaslik (USEK)

## Overview

- **Founded:** 1961 (charter); roots in the Lebanese Maronite Order scholasticate from 1938
- **Type:** Private Catholic university (Lebanese Maronite Order)
- **Education Type:** American-compatible credit/modular system, also aligned with European (ECTS-compatible)
- **Campus(es):**
  - **Main campus:** Kaslik (Jounieh)
  - **Hboub Medical Campus** (School of Medicine & Medical Sciences)
  - **Regional University Centers (RUCs):** Chekka (North), Rmeich (South), Zahlé (Beqaa)
- **Language of instruction:** English, French, and Arabic (varies by program)
- **Websites:**
  - Main: [usek.edu.lb](https://www.usek.edu.lb/)
  - Admissions: [usek.edu.lb/admission](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/admission/online-admission-requirements)
  - Apply online: [apply.usek.edu.lb](https://apply.usek.edu.lb/academic-applications)
  - Fees: [usek.edu.lb/university-fees](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/university-fees/undergraduate-studies-1)

Holy Spirit University of Kaslik was founded by the Lebanese Maronite Order in 1961, with
roots dating back to a scholasticate established in 1938. USEK serves approximately 8,000
students across 12 faculties and 3 institutes on its main Kaslik campus, a dedicated
medical campus in Hboub, and three Regional University Centers in Chekka, Rmeich, and
Zahlé. The university uses a credit-based modular system compatible with US/Canadian
standards and European ECTS. USEK offers programs in French, English, and Arabic, with
specific programs taught exclusively in one language.

| Track | Exam window | Application deadline | Reference |

### Application fees

- **Registration fee:** $200/semester (non-refundable)
- **Operational fee:** $300/semester (Fall & Spring only)
- **Initial payment:** $1,000 (includes registration)

Reference: [Undergraduate Fees](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/university-fees/undergraduate-studies-1)

### Requirements

#### English proficiency

| Test | Minimum Score | Reference |
| ---- | ------------- | --------- |
| SAT (EBRW section) | 550 | [Admission Requirements](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/admission/online-admission-requirements) |
| TOEFL iBT | 80 | [Admission Requirements](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/admission/online-admission-requirements) |
| IELTS | 6.0 | [Admission Requirements](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/admission/online-admission-requirements) |
| Duolingo | 120 | [Admission Requirements](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/admission/online-admission-requirements) |

Applicants without these scores take the USEK English placement exam.

#### French proficiency

- DELF B2 or TCF B2, **or**
- French grade ≥10/20 in Grades 10–11

Reference: [Admission Requirements](https://www.usek.edu.lb/en/admission/online-admission-requirements)

#### Program-specific exemptions

- SAT Math-based exemption for certain programs
- **Math entrance exam:** ≥70/100 to exempt
- **Engineering entrance exam:** ≥75/100 to pass

#### High-school grades

- Lebanese Baccalaureate or equivalent (IB, French Bac, etc.)
- Equivalence from the Lebanese Ministry of Education for non-Lebanese curricula
- SAT is accepted as a substitute for entrance tests in some programs
