# University of Balamand (UoB)

## Faculty

UoB offers **64 undergraduate programs** across **8 faculties + 1
institute**, distributed over five campuses: Al-Koura (main, North
Lebanon), Dekouaneh (ALBA's historic campus, near Beirut), St George
Hospital University Medical Center (Achrafieh, for FOM clinical),
Souk El Gharb – Aley, and Beino-Akkar (Issam M. Fares Faculty of
Technology). UoB follows the **American system of liberal education**;
ALBA's Dekouaneh programs additionally use the **European ECTS**
system, taught primarily in French. Each table lists the program,
degree type, home department, credits, duration (years), and language
of instruction.

### Académie Libanaise des Beaux-Arts ([ALBA](https://www.balamand.edu.lb/faculties/ALBA/Pages/default.aspx))

## Application

### Application Types + Windows

UoB runs three rounds — **Spring**, **Early Admission** (Fall), and
**Regular** (Fall). The application portal opens at
[sisweb.balamand.edu.lb](https://sisweb.balamand.edu.lb/pls/apex/f?p=100:101).
Applicants who are not granted Early Admission are automatically
forwarded to the Regular Admission pool. Admission is conditional on
the Lebanese Baccalaureate (or equivalent) being received before
registration.

| Round | Term | Closes | Decisions | Latest SAT | Reference |
| ----- | ---- | ------ | --------- | ---------- | --------- |
| Spring 2025-26 | Spring | Dec 19, 2025 | — | — | [UoB Admissions](https://www.balamand.edu.lb/AboutUOB/Pages/AdmissionsRegistration.aspx) |
| Early Admission | Fall 2026-27 | Mid-Jan 2026 | By mid-Feb 2026 | December 2025 session | [UoB Admissions](https://www.balamand.edu.lb/AboutUOB/Pages/AdmissionsRegistration.aspx) |
| Regular Admission | Fall 2026-27 | End of May 2026 | Rolling | Up to spring 2026 sessions | [UoB Admissions](https://www.balamand.edu.lb/AboutUOB/Pages/AdmissionsRegistration.aspx) |

**Difference between rounds:**

- **Early Admission** — apply by mid-January 2026 with SAT scores from
  no later than December 2025. Decisions arrive by mid-February.
  Applicants not granted Early Admission roll into the Regular pool.
- **Regular Admission** — main window, deadline end of May 2026.
  SAT/ACT scores from spring 2026 sessions are accepted.
- **Spring 2025-26** — mid-year intake; deadline December 19, 2025.

## Scholarships

### University-offered scholarships

UoB awarded over **$21 million in financial support** in AY 2024-25,
covering **3,479+ scholarship recipients**.

#### Merit scholarships

Awarded to newly admitted students based on Lebanese Baccalaureate
rank or SAT performance. Deadline: **Early Admission**.

| Coverage | Eligibility | Renewal | Reference |
| -------- | ----------- | ------- | --------- |
| **100%** | Rank 1st in Lebanese Baccalaureate (any Governorate); OR top 4 applicants ranking in top 20% of Grades 10-11 with SAT ≥ 1450 | Cumulative average ≥ 85 | [UoB Financial Support](https://www.balamand.edu.lb/CurrentStudents/FinancialSupport/Pages/FinancialSupport.aspx) |
| **75%** | Rank 2nd or 3rd in Lebanese Baccalaureate (any Governorate); OR top 6 applicants in top 20% of Grades 10-11 with SAT ≥ 1400 | Cumulative average ≥ 83 | [UoB Financial Support](https://www.balamand.edu.lb/CurrentStudents/FinancialSupport/Pages/FinancialSupport.aspx) |
| **50%** | Rank 4th or 5th in Lebanese Baccalaureate (any Governorate); OR top 10 applicants in top 20% of Grades 10-11 with SAT ≥ 1300 | Cumulative average ≥ 80 | [UoB Financial Support](https://www.balamand.edu.lb/CurrentStudents/FinancialSupport/Pages/FinancialSupport.aspx) |

**Dean's Honor List** — 100% scholarship for the following semester
for the top 4 continuing full-time undergraduates with semester
average ≥ 95; representative scholarship for full-time continuing
undergraduates with semester average ≥ 85.

## Requirements

### SAT

UoB **requires the SAT** for all undergraduate applicants except:

- Applicants to non-scientific disciplines at the Faculty of Arts and Sciences
- Applicants to ALBA
- Applicants to majors taught in French
- Applicants to Tourism and Hotel Management
- Junior/Senior transfer students with 20+ transferable credits

When registering for the SAT, use UoB code **2960**.

**Freshman minimums:**

| Track | Minimum SAT | Minimum ACT | Reference |
| ----- | ----------- | ----------- | --------- |
| Arts (Freshman) | 870 | 15 | [Admissions Guide (PDF) — Undergraduate Admission](https://www.balamand.edu.lb/Style%20Library/PDFs/ProspectiveStudents/AdmissionGuide.pdf) |
| Sciences (Freshman) | 950 | 17 | [Admissions Guide (PDF) — Undergraduate Admission](https://www.balamand.edu.lb/Style%20Library/PDFs/ProspectiveStudents/AdmissionGuide.pdf) |

### High-school grades

- A recognized secondary school certificate is required: Lebanese
  Baccalaureate, French Bac, Syrian Bac, Jordanian Tawjihi, German
  Abitur, Official Government SLC, IB Diploma (3 HL + 3 SL), or
  IGCSE/GCE (5 O-Levels + 3 A-Levels minimum, or 6 AS-Levels with
  grade ≥ D).
- An equivalence from the Lebanese Ministry of Education is required
  for non-Lebanese curricula.
- Grades 10 and 11 transcripts and recommendation must be submitted
  in a sealed envelope by the school directly, or emailed by the
  school to admissions@balamand.edu.lb.

Reference: [UoB Admissions Criteria](https://www.balamand.edu.lb/AboutUOB/Pages/AdmissionsRegistration.aspx) |

### French proficiency

French-medium programs (ALBA Dekouaneh, FAS French Language &
Literature, others marked `FR` above) require:

- DELF/DALF B2 (≥ 50/100, with ≥ 15/25 on writing) or C1 → placed in FREN201
- DELF/DALF B1 (> 70) → placed in FREN101 (must complete in first semester)
- Otherwise: pass UoB's **Test de Positionnement** (French Entrance Exam)
