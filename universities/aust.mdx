# American University of Science and Technology (AUST)

## Faculty

AUST offers **32 undergraduate programs** across 4 faculties (FAS,
FBE, FE, FHS).

Four campuses: Ashrafieh in Beirut (main), Zahlé, Sidon, and
Bhamdoun.

English is the primary language; the Faculty of Business and Economics
also runs a French Section. The American credit system applies (~30
credits/year).

AUST holds ABET accreditation for engineering and computer science,
IACBE for business, and EVALAG institutional accreditation.

## Application

### Application Types + Windows

AUST follows a **rolling-admission** model with three intakes per
academic year (Fall, Spring, Summer). Specific 2026-27 deadline dates
are published on the [AUST Academic Calendar](https://www.aust.edu.lb/section/university-calendar/fall-semester-2026-2027/2663).
Applicants who do not submit standardized test scores take the **AUST
Entrance Exam** (English Placement + Math Placement) on campus.

## Requirements

### SAT

AUST accepts **SAT** scores per the Lebanese Ministry of Education and
Higher Education (MEHE) thresholds for freshman admission:

| Track | Minimum SAT | Reference |
| ----- | ----------- | --------- |
| Arts track | 870 | [AUST SAT-I](https://www.aust.edu.lb/section/the-admission/tests-and-measurements/sat-i/217) |
| Sciences track | 950 | [AUST SAT-I](https://www.aust.edu.lb/section/the-admission/tests-and-measurements/sat-i/217) |

**SAT waiver:** Applicants without SAT scores take the **AUST Entrance
Exam** (English Placement + Math Placement Test on-campus).

> Per-faculty / per-program SAT minimums above the MEHE thresholds are
> not published publicly. Contact admissions for direct-to-major
> cutoffs.

Reference: [AUST Tests and Measurements](https://www.aust.edu.lb/section/the-admission/tests-and-measurements/210) |
[Math Placement Test](https://www.aust.edu.lb/section/the-admission/tests-and-measurements/math-placement-tests-mpt%29/213) |
[Validity of Entrance Exams](https://www.aust.edu.lb/section/the-admission/tests-and-measurements/validity-of-entrance-exams/216)

### High-school grades

- A recognized secondary school certificate is required: Lebanese
  Baccalaureate, French Bac, IB Diploma, US High School Diploma, etc.
- An equivalence from the Lebanese Ministry of Education is required
  for non-Lebanese curricula.
- Grade 11 and 12 transcripts must be submitted with the application.

Reference: [AUST Admission Requirements](https://www.aust.edu.lb/section/the-admission/overview/admission-requirements/196)

### English proficiency

All instruction at AUST is in English (with a French Section in FBE).
Applicants demonstrate proficiency by one of the following:

| Test | Accepted | Reference |
| ---- | -------- | --------- |
| TOEFL iBT | Yes | [Recognized English Tests](https://www.aust.edu.lb/section/the-admission/tests-and-measurements/recognized-english-tests/211) |
| IELTS Academic | Yes | [Recognized English Tests](https://www.aust.edu.lb/section/the-admission/tests-and-measurements/recognized-english-tests/211) |
| SAT Evidence-Based Reading & Writing | Yes | [Recognized English Tests](https://www.aust.edu.lb/section/the-admission/tests-and-measurements/recognized-english-tests/211) |
| AUST English Placement Test (EEE) | Yes | Taken on campus if no standardized test | [English Entrance Examination](https://www.aust.edu.lb/section/the-admission/graduate-programs/english-entrance-examination/204) |

> Specific minimum TOEFL / IELTS thresholds and AUST EEE placement
> bands are not published publicly. Contact admissions for current
> cutoffs.
