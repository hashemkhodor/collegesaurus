## Application

### Application Types + Windows

BAU holds **two undergraduate admission rounds per year** for Fall intake —
the **1st Round in March** and the **2nd Round in July** — followed by the
Fall semester that September. There is no separate Spring or Summer
undergraduate cycle. Applications are submitted via BAU's online portal
(the **Apply Now** button at the bottom-left of this page opens it directly).
Applicants may list **up to four programs** in order of preference on a
single application.

| Term / Round | Opens | Closes | Exam Date | Notes | Reference |
| ------------ | ----- | ------ | --------- | ----- | --------- |
| Fall 2025-26 — 1st Round | Dec 2, 2024 | Feb 26, 2025 | Mar 1, 2025 | All faculties | [Prospective Students](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Admissions/ProspectiveStudents) |
| Fall 2025-26 — 2nd Round | May 5, 2025 | Jul 17, 2025 | Jul 22, 2025 | **Medicine and Dentistry not offered** in the 2nd round | [Prospective Students](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Admissions/ProspectiveStudents) |
| Fall 2026-27 — 2nd Round | May 4, 2026 | TBA | TBA | 1st-round dates published ~Dec 2025 | [BAU Admissions](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Undergraduate/Admissions) |

**Difference between tracks:**

- **Freshman** — A 30-credit, two-semester preparatory year for students whose
  secondary certificate comes from a **non-Lebanese, 12-year system** (e.g.
  IGCSE/GCE O-levels only, IB Diploma pending equivalence). Requires SAT I
  (see Requirements below). Advances to Sophomore after completing the year
  and passing BAU's major-specific admission exam.
- **Sophomore (First Year proper)** — Direct entry for holders of the
  **Lebanese Secondary School Certificate** (any stream), Technical
  Baccalaureate, French Baccalaureate, Full IB Diploma, or other recognized
  equivalent. Must pass BAU's admission exam for the chosen major.
- **Transfer** — For students with completed credits from another recognized
  university. Requires certified transcripts and course syllabi for credit
  evaluation by the Faculty.

Each major has its own **entrance exam** that may include thinking-skills
questions, scientific subjects, a personal interview, and — for
Architecture/Design programs — a drawing test.

USD portion plus a Lebanese pound portion (roughly 65% USD / 35% LBP since

1. After registration, log into **iConnect** (`https://iconnect.bau.edu.lb`)
   and complete the financial-aid / scholarship application form for the
   relevant program (Social Aid, Work-Study, Siblings, Athletic, etc.).
2. Email the completed form to **stud-aff@bau.edu.lb** and submit hard copies
   of supporting documents to your **Faculty Executive Administrator** by
   the semester's published deadline.
3. The Financial Aid Committee reviews files, conducts an interview, and may
   send a social worker for a home inspection.
4. Approved aid is applied directly to the student's account.

Major-Based Scholarships, graduation awards) are **automatic** upon
enrollment or qualification — no application needed.

## Requirements

### SAT

The SAT I is required only for **Freshman-track** applicants (those with a
non-Lebanese 12-year secondary certificate). The minimums are set by the
Lebanese Ministry of Education Equivalence Committee, and BAU applies them
**uniformly across all faculties** — no higher faculty-level SAT threshold
is published for Medicine, Pharmacy, Dentistry, or Engineering. When
registering for the SAT, use **BAU code 7373**.

| Track | Minimum SAT I Score | Reference |
| ----- | ------------------- | --------- |
| Sciences | 950 | [Prospective Students](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Admissions/ProspectiveStudents) |
| Arts | 870 | [Prospective Students](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Admissions/ProspectiveStudents) |

**SAT waiver:** Sophomore applicants — who enter directly with a Lebanese
or equivalent 12-year secondary certificate — are **not required to submit
SAT scores**. They instead sit BAU's major-specific admission exam (which may
include thinking-skills questions, scientific subjects, a personal interview,
and — for Architecture/Design programs — a drawing test).

### High-school grades

- Official transcripts of Grades 10 and 11.
- Photocopy of Single Civil Status Record (Lebanese applicants) or
  identity card / passport (non-Lebanese applicants).
- Certified copies of official certificates or diplomas when available.
- Equivalence from the Lebanese Ministry of Education is required for
  non-Lebanese curricula before registration.

Reference: [BAU Prospective Students](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Admissions/ProspectiveStudents)

### English proficiency

All undergraduate applicants must demonstrate English proficiency by passing
BAU's English Language Admission Exam with a minimum score of **60%**, or by
submitting one of the accepted external tests below. When registering for
TOEFL, use **BAU code B516** (institutional TOEFL is **not** accepted).

| Test | Minimum Score | Notes | Reference |
| ---- | ------------- | ----- | --------- |
| BAU English Admission Exam | 60% | Administered at BAU | [Prospective Students](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Admissions/ProspectiveStudents) |
| TOEFL (paper-based, PBT) | 520 | BAU code B516 | [Prospective Students](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Admissions/ProspectiveStudents) |
| TOEFL (internet-based, iBT) | 68 | — | [Prospective Students](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Admissions/ProspectiveStudents) |
| TOEFL (computer-based, CBT) | 180 | — | [Prospective Students](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Admissions/ProspectiveStudents) |
| IELTS Academic | 5.5 | — | [Prospective Students](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Admissions/ProspectiveStudents) |
| SAT I (Reading & Writing) | 480 | Counts as English proficiency only; does not waive the Freshman SAT threshold | [Prospective Students](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Admissions/ProspectiveStudents) |
| GCE / IGCSE | Completed program | Full exemption from BAU English exam | [Prospective Students](https://www.bau.edu.lb/Admissions/ProspectiveStudents) |
| Cambridge English | Pass | Accepted per BAU FAQ | [BAU FAQ](https://www.bau.edu.lb/FAQ/Undergraduate) |

Transfer students from English-medium institutions (≥2 years of study) are
also exempted. Applicants who do not meet the threshold must enroll in BAU's
Intensive English Program through the Language Centre **before** any other
courses.
