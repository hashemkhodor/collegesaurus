# Hariri Foundation for Sustainable Human Development

## Overview
- **Provider:** [Hariri Foundation for Sustainable Human Development](https://hariri-foundation.org/)
- **Type:** Lebanese private non-governmental foundation (member of [UN ECOSOC](https://hariri-foundation.org/story/) with special consultative status)
- **Founded:** 1979, by the late Prime Minister [Rafic Hariri](https://hfusa.org/?page_id=57)
- **Current President:** H.E. Bahia Hariri ([source](https://hariri-foundation.org/story/))
- **US Branch:** [Hariri Foundation-USA (HFUSA)](https://hfusa.org/), established 1985

The Hariri Foundation was created during Lebanon's civil war (1975-1990) with the conviction that "education holds the key to Lebanon's future" ([source](https://hfusa.org/?page_id=30)). Since 1979 the foundation has awarded **35,992 full scholarships** to students from across Lebanon, enabling them to pursue undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate studies at **1,766 universities and institutions across 27 countries**, with a cumulative investment of approximately **$1.5 billion** ([source](https://hariri-foundation.org/programs/university-scholarships/)). In addition, the foundation continues to grant **partial scholarships** to hundreds of university students at Lebanon's private universities and the Lebanese University ([source](https://hariri-foundation.org/programs/education-scholarships/)).

The foundation also operates five schools, one university (Hariri Canadian University), one technical institute, a CISCO Networking Academy, and 17 primary health centers across Lebanon ([source](https://hfusa.org/?page_id=30)).

## Grade & background requirements
- **Academic level:** Undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate (varies by track) ([source](https://hariri-foundation.org/programs/education-scholarships/))
- **GPA / Baccalaureate average:** Selection is based on **merit and financial need**; specific GPA thresholds are not publicly listed ([source](https://hariri-foundation.org/programs/education-scholarships/))
- **Nationality / residency:** Lebanese students from across Lebanon ([source](https://hariri-foundation.org/programs/university-scholarships/))
- **Financial need:** Required — financial need is explicitly part of the selection criteria for school-level and university-level aid ([source](https://hariri-foundation.org/programs/education-scholarships/))
- **Other (HFUSA):** Many US-bound scholars complete intensive English language training before starting their degree, with programs hosted at Boston University, University of Arizona, and University of South Florida ([source](https://hfusa.org/?page_id=312))

## Supported universities

**Lebanon-based scholarships:** Private universities across Lebanon, the Lebanese University, and Rafik Hariri University (RHU) ([source](https://hariri-foundation.org/programs/education-scholarships/), [source](https://v2.hariri-foundation.org/scholarships-to-28-students-awarded-by-the-hariri-foundation-and-rafik-hariri-university/)).

**HFUSA sponsorship (US/Canada):** Over **300 institutions** including MIT, Harvard, Columbia, Yale, Stanford, Cornell, University of Pennsylvania, Johns Hopkins, Georgetown, and Boston University ([source](https://hfusa.org/?page_id=312)).

**Most popular fields of study** (HFUSA track):
- Engineering (over 50% of graduates — especially electrical and civil engineering)
- Social Sciences (business, management, economics, communications, political science)
- Health Sciences (M.D., D.D.S., Ph.D., pharmacy, public health)
- Computer science, mathematics, architecture, law, education, and biological sciences

([source](https://hfusa.org/?page_id=312))

## Benefits

### Full scholarships (Lebanon-based, historic program)
- Tuition fees
- Foreign language training
- Travel expenses
- Accommodation
- Necessary academic equipment

([source](https://hariri-foundation.org/programs/university-scholarships/))

### Partial scholarships (Lebanon-based, ongoing)
- Partial tuition coverage at Lebanese private universities and the Lebanese University ([source](https://hariri-foundation.org/programs/education-scholarships/))

### HFUSA student sponsorship (US/Canada)
- **Interest-free loans** covering tuition and related expenses ([source](https://hfusa.org/?page_id=26))
- Repayment is expected after completing academic training; monthly payment plans are available
- A binding legal contract is required, but repayment terms are described as flexible ([source](https://hfusa.org/?page_id=26))
- Repaid funds are recycled to support future educational programs in Lebanon ([source](https://hfusa.org/?page_id=26))

### Georgetown fellowships
- Hariri Fellowship (MBA): Merit-based award at Georgetown McDonough School of Business ([source](https://hfusa.org/?page_id=21))
- Saad Hariri Endowed Scholarship: Need-based aid covering up to four years of undergraduate study ([source](https://hfusa.org/?page_id=21))

<div className="alert-warning">

**Loan vs. Grant** — The HFUSA sponsorship program operates as an **interest-free loan**, not an outright grant. Students are expected to repay after graduation, though the foundation describes repayment as carrying both "legal and moral understanding" ([source](https://hfusa.org/?page_id=26)). The Lebanon-based full and partial scholarships appear to be grants with no repayment obligation.

</div>
