# Tomooh Scholarship Fund (PepsiCo × Ajialouna)

## Overview

- **Provider (funder):** [PepsiCo International](https://www.middleeast.pepsico.com/our-stories/story/pepsico-s-tomooh-scholarship-program)
  with co-funding via the PepsiCo Foundation and SMLC (Société Moderne
  Libanaise pour le Commerce — PepsiCo's exclusive Lebanese bottler since 1952).
- **Administrator:** [Ajialouna Organization](https://ajialouna.org/) — a
  Lebanese NGO founded 1995 by Dr. Lina Zaim Dada. Ajialouna runs the
  application process, selects awardees, and disburses funds directly
  to the universities.
- **Type:** Private corporate-NGO partnership (named scholarship fund).
- **Founded:** Pledged in **2003** by then-PepsiCo CEO for Asia/MENA/Africa
  Saad Abdul-Latif as a $1M commitment to Arab youth education;
  **launched in Lebanon in 2006**. Renewed/expanded at AUB in **September 2020**.
- **Annual funding:** Currently approximately **USD $250,000 / year**
  (scaled up from $200K). By the program's 14th year (~2020) PepsiCo
  had funded **3,000+ Lebanese scholarships** since launch
  ([Executive Bulletin](https://executive-bulletin.com/ngo/tomooh-saad-abdul-latif-for-university-scholarships-more-than-3000-grants-given-to-students-in-lebanon-in-14th-year-running)).
- **Status:** **Active for AY 2025-26** — applications continue with a
  June 30 annual deadline ([Ajialouna — Tomooh](https://ajialouna.org/tomooh/)).
- **Description:** A region-wide partial-tuition scholarship for
  financially disadvantaged but academically deserving Lebanese youth
  ages 18–22. Awards are paid directly to the host institution. The
  program is open to **all majors and faculties** across **13 private
  and public Lebanese universities** including AUB, LAU, and BAU.

## Grade & background requirements

- **Age:** 18–22 years old at time of application
  ([Ajialouna — Tomooh](https://ajialouna.org/tomooh/)).
- **Academic level:** Current university students enrolled in (or admitted
  to) one of the participating Lebanese universities. Both incoming
  freshmen and continuing students are eligible.
- **Academic performance:** Must demonstrate **outstanding academic
  performance** ([PepsiCo MENA](https://www.middleeast.pepsico.com/our-stories/story/pepsico-s-tomooh-scholarship-program)).
- **Financial need:** Required — applicants must demonstrate genuine
  financial need supported by documentation.
- **Nationality / gender / ability:** Open to all students "irrespective
  of nationality, gender, race or special needs"
  ([PepsiCo MENA](https://www.middleeast.pepsico.com/our-stories/story/pepsico-s-tomooh-scholarship-program)).
  In AY 2023-24, 32 of the 177 Lebanon recipients were students with
  special needs ([LAU news](https://news.lau.edu.lb/2025/tomooh-scholarship-students-spotlighted-at-lau.php)).
- **Major / faculty restrictions:** None — covers all majors across all
  participating universities.

## Application window

- **Application route:** **Direct to Ajialouna** — applications are
  submitted to and reviewed by the Ajialouna Organization, NOT the
  university financial-aid offices. Selected awardees are then coordinated
  with each university for direct tuition disbursement.
- **Deadline:** **June 30 annually**
  ([Ajialouna — Tomooh](https://ajialouna.org/tomooh/)).
- **How to apply:** Email **Tomooh@ajialouna.org** for the application
  form, or call Ajialouna's main office (+961 1 30 44 85). Online
  application is via the [Tomooh page on ajialouna.org](https://ajialouna.org/tomooh/).

## Benefits

The Tomooh scholarship is a **partial-tuition** award — not a full ride
— and is paid **directly to the host institution** (the student does
not receive a cash transfer).

| Benefit | Coverage | Reference |
| ------- | -------- | --------- |
| Tuition | **USD $500 – $5,000 per student per year** (scaled to financial need) | [Takadam — Tomooh](https://takadam.com/opportunity/tomooh/) |
| Disbursement | Paid directly to the participating university | [Ajialouna — Education](https://ajialouna.org/education/) |
| Renewal | Renewable annually at the discretion of PepsiCo / Ajialouna; requires continued financial need + academic standing | [Takadam — Tomooh](https://takadam.com/opportunity/tomooh/) |
| Stipend / housing | **Not included** per public sources — Tomooh is tuition-only | [PepsiCo MENA](https://www.middleeast.pepsico.com/our-stories/story/pepsico-s-tomooh-scholarship-program) |

### Related: Ajialouna's standalone scholarship

Separately from the Tomooh joint program, **Ajialouna runs its own
"Ajialouna Scholarship"** established in **October 2021** and
independently funded. It supports university tuition for students
demonstrating financial need and good academic standing. As of the
program's anniversary, [Ajialouna and the Tomooh program had together
supported over 12,500 Lebanese students](https://ajialouna.org/presidents-scholarship-reception-2025-on-april-11/).
For details on the standalone scholarship see
[ajialouna.org/education](https://ajialouna.org/education/) or contact
Ajialouna at admin@ajialouna.org.
