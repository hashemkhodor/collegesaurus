# Stipendium Hungaricum Scholarship Programme

## Overview

- **Provider:** [Hungarian Government](https://stipendiumhungaricum.hu/about/), Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade; administered by the [Tempus Public Foundation](https://tpf.hu/)
- **Type:** Government-funded scholarship (bilateral agreement-based)
- **Founded:** 2013, as part of Hungary's "Eastern and Southern Opening" policy
- **Description:** The Stipendium Hungaricum Scholarship Programme provides fully funded
  opportunities for Lebanese students to pursue **Bachelor's, Master's, One-Tier Master's,
  Doctoral, or non-degree** studies at Hungarian universities. The programme aims to increase
  the number of international students in Hungary and strengthen academic ties between Hungary
  and over 100 partner countries. Around **900 study programmes** are offered, primarily in
  English, across 30+ Hungarian higher education institutions. Lebanese applicants must be
  nominated by the **Ministry of Education and Higher Education (MEHE)** — direct applications
  are not accepted.

## Grade & background requirements

- **Nationality:** Lebanese citizen.
- **Academic level:**
  - **Bachelor's / One-Tier Master's:** At least 12 years of completed education and a secondary school diploma (Baccalaureate)
  - **Master's:** A completed Bachelor's degree
  - **Doctoral (PhD):** A completed Master's degree
- **GPA:** No fixed minimum GPA is published. The programme requires "excellent school achievements" and "good academic standing." Competitive applicants typically have strong grades.
- **Age:** Must be at least 18 years old by 31 August of the academic year start. No upper age limit set by the central programme.
- **English proficiency:** Most programmes are taught in English. Applicants must provide an official language proficiency certificate (TOEFL, IELTS, or equivalent) meeting the minimum set by the chosen programme — generally at least **B2 level**. Alternatively, proof that previous studies were conducted fully in English is accepted.
- **Hungarian language:** All scholarship holders must complete a mandatory **"Hungarian as a Foreign Language"** course in their first year. For programmes taught in Hungarian, a one-year preparatory language course is included.
- **Nomination:** Lebanese applicants **must** be nominated by the Ministry of Education and Higher Education (MEHE). Direct applications without MEHE nomination are not accepted.
- **Other:**
  - Applicants may select up to **2 study programme choices**

Over **30 Hungarian higher education institutions** participate, offering approximately 900 programmes primarily in English. The full, up-to-date list is at [apply.stipendiumhungaricum.hu/institutions](https://apply.stipendiumhungaricum.hu/institutions).

- Óbuda University

## Benefits

The scholarship covers the **full duration** of the programme, 12 months per year:

| Benefit | Coverage | Reference |
| ------- | -------- | --------- |
| Tuition | 100% waiver — full exemption from tuition fees | [Stipendium Hungaricum — About](https://stipendiumhungaricum.hu/about/) |
| Monthly stipend (Bachelor's / Master's / One-Tier) | HUF 43,700/month (~EUR 110) | [Stipendium Hungaricum — About](https://stipendiumhungaricum.hu/about/) |
| Monthly stipend (Doctoral — Phase 1, semesters 1-4) | HUF 140,000/month (~EUR 355) | [Stipendium Hungaricum — About](https://stipendiumhungaricum.hu/about/) |
| Monthly stipend (Doctoral — Phase 2, semesters 5-8) | HUF 180,000/month (~EUR 460) | [Stipendium Hungaricum — About](https://stipendiumhungaricum.hu/about/) |
| Accommodation | Free dormitory placement, **or** HUF 40,000/month (~EUR 100) housing contribution | [University of Pécs — SH](https://international.pte.hu/stipendium-hungaricum-scholarship-program) |
| Health insurance | Hungarian public healthcare + supplementary private insurance up to HUF 65,000/year | [Stipendium Hungaricum — About](https://stipendiumhungaricum.hu/about/) |
| Visa fee | Exemption from student visa application fees | [Stipendium Hungaricum — About](https://stipendiumhungaricum.hu/about/) |
| Language course | Mandatory "Hungarian as a Foreign Language" course included | [Stipendium Hungaricum — About](https://stipendiumhungaricum.hu/about/) |

**Note:** The stipend is officially described as "a contribution to living expenses" and **does not fully cover** all costs of living in Hungary, especially in Budapest. Students should expect to supplement with personal funds. Travel costs to/from Hungary are **not** covered by the programme — check with MEHE whether the sending partner provides travel support.
