# External Scholarships — Overview

This tab covers **external scholarships** — funding offered by NGOs, private
foundations, and government programs that students in Lebanon can apply to *in
addition* to any aid offered by their university. (Scholarships offered *by* a
specific university live on that university's page in the
[Universities](/universities/intro) tab.)

## How each scholarship page is organized

Every scholarship page follows the same six sections so you can compare
opportunities quickly:

1. **Overview** — provider, type, and a one-paragraph description.
2. **Grade & background requirements** — academic level, GPA, nationality /
   residency, financial need, and any demographic or major restrictions.
3. **Application window** — when applications open, when they close, and the
   decision timeline.
4. **Supported universities** — which universities the scholarship can be used
   at.
5. **Benefits** — what is covered (tuition %, housing, stipend, books, travel,
   mentorship).
6. **Contacts of recipients** — past recipients who agreed to answer questions,
   plus the official program contact. This is the most useful section: real
   people who have been through the process and can give you honest advice.
