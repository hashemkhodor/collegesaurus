# LIFE Scholarship

## Overview
- **Provider:** LIFE (Lebanese International Finance Executives) — a non-partisan, non-sectarian, independent membership organization
- **Type:** Need-based + merit-based scholarship from an international NGO
- **Founded:** 2009
- **Description:** LIFE provides university scholarships to outstanding Lebanese students
  (or those of Lebanese descent) who demonstrate financial need and academic excellence.
  The program combines tuition support with mentoring, career guidance, and soft-skills
  training through the LIFE Academy. Since 2009, LIFE has awarded over 1,100 scholarships
  to Lebanese students studying in Lebanon and abroad.
- **Official link:** [lifelebanon.com/scholarship](https://www.lifelebanon.com/scholarship/life)

References: [Myriad Canada — LIFE](https://www.myriadcanada.org/en/project/scholarships-for-lebanese-students-with-life/) |
[AUB — LIFE impact story](https://boldly.aub.edu.lb/impact-stories/life-scholarship-provides-life-changing-opportunities-for-aub-students/)

## Grade & background requirements
- **Nationality:** Lebanese or of Lebanese descent
- **Academic level:** Undergraduate (Lebanon universities) and Graduate (abroad universities)
- **Academic record:** At or above the **80th percentile**, maintained throughout enrollment
- **Financial need:** Demonstrable financial need (required)
- **Fields of study:** Economics, Finance, Engineering, Computer Science, Mathematics, Corporate Law, and STEM fields
- **Personal qualities:** Drive and ambition to become a leader in your field

Reference: [Bachelorsportal — LIFE Scholarship](https://www.bachelorsportal.com/scholarships/8480/life-scholarship.html)

**Application process:**
1. Submit application online at [lifelebanon.com/scholarship](https://www.lifelebanon.com/scholarship)
2. Applications with missing information or documents will not be considered
3. Once submitted, you cannot edit fields or attach additional documents
4. Shortlisted candidates are invited for an initial screening interview
5. Selected candidates proceed to a second interview with a LIFE member
6. Interview invitations are communicated via email

## Benefits
- **Tuition (Lebanon):** Up to **$6,000/year** (up to $4,000/year per some sources; amounts may vary by university partnership)
- **Tuition (abroad):** Up to **$12,000/year**
- **LIFE Academy:** Training in soft skills and industry-relevant skills
- **Mentoring:** Dedicated professional mentor from LIFE's global membership network
- **Career support:** Internship placement assistance, job counseling, and networking opportunities with accomplished finance and business professionals
- **Mental health support:** Available to scholars
- **Alumni commitment:** Scholars commit to funding future LIFE scholarships after graduating
