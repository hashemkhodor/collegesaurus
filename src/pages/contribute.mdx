# Contribute

Collegesaurus is maintained by volunteers. Anyone can help keep it
accurate. You don't need to know how to code, and most paths take less
than five minutes.

Pick whichever matches what you want to share:

## Something on a page is wrong

Numbers go out of date, deadlines shift, contacts move. You don't have to
fix it yourself. Just tell us what's wrong and where you saw the correct
info.

[**Suggest a correction →**](https://github.com/hashemkhodor/collegesaurus/issues/new?template=inaccurate-info.yml)

## A scholarship or university we're missing

If you know of a scholarship available to Lebanese students, or a
university that should be on the site:

- [**Suggest a new scholarship →**](https://github.com/hashemkhodor/collegesaurus/issues/new?template=new-scholarship.yml)
- [**Suggest a new university →**](https://github.com/hashemkhodor/collegesaurus/issues/new?template=new-university.yml)

The more links to official pages you can include (tuition rates,
application form, FAQ), the faster we can publish it.

## Write a story

The [Stories section](/stories) is where students publish their own
first-hand experiences as full posts: how the application really went,
what they paid, what the interview asked, what they wish they'd known.

There are two ways to get yours up there:

1. **Fill in [Share your experience](https://github.com/hashemkhodor/collegesaurus/issues/new?template=share-experience.yml)**
   and we'll turn your answers into a proper story with your name (or
   anonymous, your call).
2. **Write it yourself**: copy
   [`stories/_template.md`](https://github.com/hashemkhodor/collegesaurus/blob/main/stories/_template.md),
   rename it to `YYYY-MM-DD-your-slug.md`, add yourself to
   [`stories/authors.yml`](https://github.com/hashemkhodor/collegesaurus/blob/main/stories/authors.yml),
   and open a PR. Every existing story uses the same shape so you can
   crib from any of them.

## You can write the fix yourself

Every university and scholarship page has an **"Edit this page"** link at
the very bottom. Clicking it opens GitHub's in-browser editor for the
exact source file. GitHub handles the fork and pull request for you. No
local setup, no command line.

## No GitHub account?

Email **hashemkhoder1@gmail.com** with the subject line
`Collegesaurus contribution` and the same info you'd have filled into the
form. We'll file the issue for you.

## What happens next

- The maintainer reviews the issue within a few days.
- If the info is verifiable, the page gets updated and the site
  redeploys within about an hour of the merge.
- You're credited in the commit message unless you ask otherwise.

Thank you. Every correction, however small, helps a student make a
better-informed decision.
